SE Support Services List

  • HW BOM Validation: Perform customer/prospects existing hardware compatibility check on AHV, also recommends component replacement of the existing HW to meet the AHV compatibility.
  • Architectural Diagram Creation: Creation of Technical Architectural Diagrams to visualize the Current vs Future Proposed Infrastructure and share with the account SE.
  • Data Center Info Collection: Collects customer/prospects existing infra data collection and share with account team to create sizing BOM.
  • 3rd Party App Validation (Soft Launch): Currently under soft launch, assist SE to validate 3rd party apps compatibility on AHV and initiate app validation prioritization by partnering with the Nutanix Alliances and SE Management.
  • Customer Success Support (Soft Launch): Part of a soft launch, SE Support team are going to assist customer success sales team on resolving few pre-identified technical queries raised by the existing customers. SE Support team is involved only when the account SE raises the SE Support case for assistance.
  • Request Product Enhancement (Soft Launch): Currently under soft launch, SEs can now submit a SE Support case to propose product new feature enhancements. This will be prioritized based on the associated opportunity ACV value and then shared with the respective PMs to expedite the feature enhancement.
